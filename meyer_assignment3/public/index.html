Ten Albums for The Spring

10. Kanye West - My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy

Made by Kanye West is album is a fan-favorite and a classic in my books as Mr. West leans into some of his darker, but more versatile material.

9. The Strokes - Is This It

The Strokes make a splashing debut on their debut record and takes ahold of the listener and provides an experience they will never forget.

8. Fleetwood Mac - Rumors

In the midst of turmoil Fleetwood Mac makes a legendary record that stands the test of time and cements their place in rock and music forever.

7. Kids See Ghosts - Kids See Ghosts

Kid Cudi and Kanye West team up on this collab album that tells a tale of their turmoil, past, and recovery towards light and hope.

6. Gorillaz - Plastic Beach

Gorillaz attack the concept of pollution with this slick studio album that offers a variety of musical stylings and top-notch guest features.

5. Red Hot Chili Peppers - Californication

The Red Hot Chili Peppers continued their streak of popularity in the music and rock scene with this studio album that is energetic, bold, and colorful.

4. Prince - 1999

Prince offers up several stellar tracks in this classic funk and pop album that will make you want to get up and dance.

3. Travis Scott - Astroworld

Rapper Travis Scott soars above all the talent with his third solo studio album which offers experimental tracks and incredible guest features that are out of this world.

2. Twenty One Pilots - Trench

The duo of Twenty One Pilots dives deep into their own mythology and ideas with this conceptual album that draws upon a dystopian society in the future.

1. J. Cole - 2014 Forest Hills Drive

J. Cole evolves in this classic rap and hip-hop projects where his lyricism is as varied and versatile as his production and the bars and flows never stop coming your way.
